# Product Vision

## Why am I building this?

This project has two purposes:

1. To gain a practical experience in software development by building a real world application as part of my CS learning.
2. To solve my own personal finance problem. I currently track my expenses, loans, savings and monthly summaries in Notion. This application will provide a dedicated solution that is faster, more organized and easier to expand over time.

The long term goal is to evolve it into complete personal finance application with additional features and automation.

## Target user

- __Version 1:__ Myself
- **Future:** Anyone who wants to manage their personal finances.

## Problem statement

Managing personal finances across expenses, savings and loans is time consuming and difficult to analyze. Users need a simple way to record transactions and understand their financial activity through monthly summaries.

## Goals (Minimum Viable Product)

- Record daily financial transactions (Cash, Bank Account, ATM card, Jazz Cash).
- Categorize transactions as expenses, savings or loans.
- View transaction history of every month.
- Generate monthly financial summaries.

## Non-goals(Version 1)

- Bank Integration
- Automatic transaction import
- Multiple currencies
- Shared or team finance management
- AI powered categorization or automation
- Send email for every month summary
