# Domain glossary

## Account

An account represents a place where money is held or managed.

Examples:

- Cash Wallet
- Bank account(Saving,current)
- JazzCash

## Transaction

A transaction is any financial event that changes the balance of account.

Examples:

- Income
- Expense
- Transfer
- Loan Given
- Loan Repaid

### Does this event immediately increase or decrease my total wealth?

It depends whether transaction happen inside or outside direction.

## Income

Income is money added to account from external source that increase the wealth.

Examples:

- Salary
- Eide
- Gift
- Bonus

### Does this event immediately increase or decrease my total wealth?

Increase the wealth

## Expense

An expense is money spent from an account to obtain goods, services.

Examples:

- Food
- Home groceries
- Rent
- Fee

### Does this event immediately increase or decrease my total wealth?

Decrease the wealth

## Transfer

A transfer moves money b/w two accounts without changing total wealth.

- Cash → Bank
- Bank → JazzCash
- Saving → Current Account

### Does this event immediately increase or decrease my total wealth?

Neutral

## Loan

A loan is a money borrowed or lent with the expectation that it will be repaid.

### Loan Given

Your definition: When lent money to someone.

#### Does this event immediately increase or decrease my total wealth?

Neutral initially. Money leaves an account but a loan receivable is created.

---

### Loan Borrowed

Your definition: When borrowed money from someone.

#### Does this event immediately increase or decrease my total wealth?

Neutral initially. Money enters an account, but a loan liability is created.

## Asset

Something valuable that I own or have the right to receive.

Examples:

- Money
- Gold
- Money someone owes me

## Liability

Money or another financial obligation that I owe to someone else.

Examples:

- Unpaid bill
- Credit card balance
- Loan borrowed

## Balance

The amount of money currently held in a specific account.

Examples:

- Cash Wallet balance: PKR 5,000
- Bank Account balance: PKR 40,000
- JazzCash balance: PKR 2,000

## Net Wealth

The total value of everything I own, minus everything I owe.

Net Wealth = Total Assets - Total Liabilities
